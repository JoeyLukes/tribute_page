PC Specs

The Specifications of your PC are very important for the technical side of your setup. Will you be gaming competitvely or casually? Will you just be using it for work? The main PC parts include but are not limited to:

  • Processor (CPU)
  • MotherBoard
  • Graphics Card
  • Storage (Hardrive or SSD)
  • RAM
  • Cooling System
  • Power Supply
  • Case

Peripherals

A common misconception is that the keyboard, mouse, headset, and monitor are apart of your computer. In reality, they are accessories separate from your computer that allow you to use it. The most common peripherals are:

  • Keybaord
  • Mouse
  • Monitor(s)
  • Headset
  • Microphone
  • Speakers

The Setup

The Setup is what is going to make your room pop. This is where your actual PC, your Peripherals, and the rest of the room tie together. Many times, a color scheme is chosen and LED lights are put behind the monitors and desk to tie the color of the room together. If you want an all white theme, you might want to pick a white Case for your PC, as well as some white Peripherals like a white mouse or keyboard.
